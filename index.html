01 — About Me

From the Himalayas
to Permian Basin IT

I was raised in Nepal, surrounded by the Himalayas — a place that teaches you to think in systems and respect complexity. I brought that mindset to West Texas, where I've spent the last several years transforming how a university operates at the technology layer.

As a Business & Application Analyst at UTPB, I sit at the intersection of institutional leadership and enterprise technology. I translate strategy into working systems — designing workflows, integrating platforms, and automating processes that serve thousands of students and faculty every day.

I believe great technology should be invisible: reliable, intuitive, and so well-designed that no one has to think about it. My job is making that happen across the institution.

Outside of work I'm drawn to data science, machine learning, and geospatial analysis — fields that let me keep exploring the edge of what's possible with data.
